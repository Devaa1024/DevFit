Overall
Score weighted across 7 signals — Overload 22% · Diet 23% · Bodyweight 17% · Body fat 10% · Sleep 10% · Steps 10% · Stress 8%. Untracked signals are skipped and the rest re-normalise automatically. Recomp credit applies if body-fat drops while weight stalls.
Bodyweight is scored on weekly rate of change: loss 0.5–1%/wk (lean-mass sparing — Helms 2014), lean gain 0.25–0.5%/wk (Aragon & Schoenfeld 2013), maintain ±0.5%/wk. Steps target 8–10k/day (Paluch 2022, Lancet); sleep 7–9h (AASM/NSF).
