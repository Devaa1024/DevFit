How DevFit works
◉
Progress (home)Log bodyweight, steps and sleep each day. The Today Overview shows them vs your goals, and Training Status tells you if you're recovered enough to train (from last night's sleep) and which routine is next.
▦
TrainingBuild your plan in Edit Plan (add strength or cardio moves), log each session under Log Session, and track lifts over time in Progress. Generate a branded share card or program PDF.
🍎
NutritionUse the macro calculator (Mifflin-St Jeor / Katch-McArdle + activity) to set calorie & macro targets, then log food against them in the daily diary. Search a large food database by name or barcode.
★
Score & Analytics (Pro)A weekly True Progress Score blends bodyweight direction, overload, diet, sleep, steps and stress with world-standard weights. Charts show your trend once you've logged a little data.
⤬
Share cardsWorkout, cardio and weekly-progress cards in light, dark or transparent themes. Set your @handle and card font below under Personal → Share Card.
☁
Cloud syncEverything saves on this device and syncs to your account automatically, so logging in on another phone restores your data. No buttons to press.

Install DevFit on iPhone
1. Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari (square with up arrow).

2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

3. Tap Add in the top right.

DevFit will appear on your home screen like a native app.
